Krishnagiri: A school student from the district was felicitated by the collector here on Wednesday for the bagging the 3rd spot in a state level chess competition.

Yokesh Srenevas R of Sri Vijay Vidhyalaya Mtriculation High School bagged the third place in under-18 state level chess competition organised by the school Education Department at Doveton Corrie Anglo-Indian Higher Secondary School in Chennai on Children's Day(November 14)

Yokesh was fecilitated by Collector T P Rajesh on Wednesday. District Sports Officer Sivakumar was present among others.
